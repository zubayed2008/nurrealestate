About Us

We are

AK Nur International Real Estate Agency is a reliable organization that helps you to buying and selling your property. The main motto of the agency is to connecting the property owner with the real buyer and seller with integrity and utmost sincerity. This agency attaches great importance and transparency to your connection between the actual buyer and seller, freeing you from many hassle and different kind of unwanted situation. It also helps you to save your valuable time and labor. While serving you, the agency does not sell the property in any way higher than the price set by the seller, i.e. the price at which the property seller wants to sell his/her property, the agency tells the buyer the same price. For example if a flat seller wants to sell his/her flat by one crore taka, the agency will try its best to sell the flat for one crore taka and the agency will arrange for the buyer to contact the flat owner directly as the price of the flat is one crore taka. The agency doesn't hide any information or resort to deception in any way. The agency will not interfere with the price in any way if the buyer can buy the flat at any price after talking to the owner. The agency's job is to bridge the gap between the actual buyer and the seller in exchange for a service charge.
